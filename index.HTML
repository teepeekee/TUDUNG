ABOUT

Naelofar was established in 2015 by an entrepreneur, Nur Izzati Binti Muhammad Shuib. Her experience in the Muslim fashion inspired her and eventually got very famous and outran other Muslim fashion industry. Miss Izzati also took her to review global Muslim fashion such as the United Kingdom, France, Italy, Japan, Indonesia, Singapore, and others. This journey has helped and inspired us to come up with the design under our own brand.
